Locale

See where you actually dine – a map of your restaurant spending.

What is Locale?

Locale is an iOS app that connects to your bank or credit card accounts via Plaid and visualizes your U.S. restaurant transactions on a map over time.

Key features

  • Securely connect one or more accounts via Plaid
  • Fetch up to 12 months of historical transactions
  • Filter to restaurant-only spend (excluding coffee shops, bars, and fast food)
  • View where you’ve eaten on an interactive map
  • All data stored locally on your device

Data & security

Locale uses Plaid to handle bank connections and credentials. Your login details are never seen or stored by Locale.
